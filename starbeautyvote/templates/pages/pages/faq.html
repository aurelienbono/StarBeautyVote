Platform, Public Voting

What is StarBeautyVote and how does it work for public voting?

StarBeautyVote is an electronic voting platform that allows the public to participate in online contests in a secure and transparent way. Users can create an account, browse the available contests, select their favorite candidates and vote.

What measures does StarBeautyVote take to ensure transparent voting?

We guarantee the transparency of the voting process through secure methods and rigorous audits. Every vote is verifiably recorded to ensure the integrity and fairness of the results.

What payment methods are accepted on StarBeautyVote?

We accept mobile money and credit card payments to facilitate transactions on the platform.

Dashboard for Promoters

How do I register as a promoter on StarBeautyVote?

To become a promoter on StarBeautyVote, you need to create an account by providing your basic information. Once registered, you will have access to a personalized dashboard to manage your contests.

How do I create a competition on StarBeautyVote?

Once logged in to your promoter account, you can create a new competition by filling in the required details such as competition name, start and end dates, description, category, etc.

How do I create or share the registration link for candidates?

On your promoter dashboard, you can generate a unique registration link for candidates. This link can be shared via email, social networks, or any other relevant communication method.

SMS campaigns, AI support and other features

How can I benefit from SMS campaigns on StarBeautyVote?

Users can use our SMS campaign service to promote their contests and increase participation. This feature is available in our Business Plan.

How can I get AI support on StarBeautyVote?

StarBeautyVote offers AI support for strategic advice and user experience optimization. Users can access this feature to improve the management of their contests and maximize their impact.

How can I get technical support to use the platform?

Each subscription plan includes 6 months of technical support. For further assistance, you can contact our dedicated team via our online support or by e-mail.
